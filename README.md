# 📊 Comprehensive Analysis and Dietary Strategies with Tableau  
## A College Food Choices Case Study

A data analytics and visualization project that analyzes student food choices, dietary habits, and nutritional behavior using **Tableau dashboards**.  
This project focuses on generating actionable insights to improve dietary awareness in college environments.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze student dietary patterns and identify factors influencing food choices such as:

- Lifestyle habits  
- Academic pressure  
- Emotional behavior  
- Childhood food influence  
- Nutritional awareness  

The goal is to provide **data-driven insights for healthier campus food planning**.

---

## 📌 Key Features

- Interactive Tableau dashboards for data visualization  
- Analysis of student lifestyle and eating behavior  
- Nutritional awareness and health trend insights  
- Emotional and psychological influence on food choices  
- Data-driven storytelling for decision making  

---

## 📊 Dashboards Included

### 1. Lifestyle Analysis
- Gender distribution  
- GPA and academic performance trends  
- Employment status of students  

### 2. Dietary Behavior Analysis
- Breakfast habits  
- Comfort food preferences  
- Cuisine selection patterns  
- Calorie awareness levels  

### 3. Health & Nutrition Analysis
- Exercise frequency  
- Vitamin intake  
- Fruit and vegetable consumption  
- Self-reported health status  

### 4. Behavioral Influence Analysis
- Childhood food habits  
- Emotional eating patterns  
- Stress-based food consumption  

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

- Stress and academic pressure strongly influence eating habits  
- Childhood food habits impact adult food choices  
- Many students have low nutritional awareness  
- Convenience and emotional factors dominate food decisions
